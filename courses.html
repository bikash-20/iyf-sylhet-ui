Life-skills course for youth

Be SMART

A five-part course built around Simplicity, Modesty, Awareness, Regularity, and Truthfulness — practical grounding for young devotees, drawn from Krishna conscious teaching.

Format
Weekly, in person
Duration
5 weeks
Who it's for
Youth & college students
Where
Radha Madhava Mandir
Course modules

Five weeks, five principles

S

Simplicity

Learning to live with less noise and fewer distractions, so attention has somewhere real to go.

M

Modesty

Practicing humility in speech and conduct, on and off the mandir grounds.

A

Awareness

Building the habit of noticing — one's own mind, one's surroundings, and one's choices.

R

Regularity

Establishing a steady daily practice: japa, study, and service that doesn't depend on mood.

T

Truthfulness

Honesty with others and with oneself, as the foundation everything else stands on.
